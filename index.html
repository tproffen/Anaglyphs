What are anaglyphs?

We see the world in 3D, because each eye sees a slightly different picture. The two images are then processes by our brain to produce our 3D view of the world. Anaglyphs use filters, to separate two different images encoded in a picture or movie to creat the same 3D effect. In most cinemas, the to images are encoded by different polarization of light and the cool 3D glasses you wear, separate the images for each eye. Using glasses like the one's on the left allow separating parts of an image by color in a red and cyan part for each eye. The tools belo allow one to draw in 3D and using two webcams, take picture in 3D right from the browser.
